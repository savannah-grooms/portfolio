FEATURED POST

A dynamic force in journalism, Savannah Grooms has a wide range of experience, from participating in the prestigious POLITICO Journalism Institute to her current role as the youngest intern ever at AARP Maryland. There she specializes in advocacy efforts and communications.

As a Banneker Key Scholar at the University of Maryland, she is passionate about diversity and inclusion and serves as the student representative of DEI at her school of journalism. Additionally, Savannah moderates for the League of Women Voters in Prince George's County, spreading information about candidates to her hometown county. Savannah was a previous reporter for The Diamondback and the Bowie Banner.

Savannah hopes to help news deserts and spread news to vulnerable areas. With her drive for excellence, she hopes to make a significant impact in media and beyond.
